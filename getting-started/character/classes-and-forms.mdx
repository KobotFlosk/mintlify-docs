
Character class and character form play a role in determining compatibility and other processes or perks within the system.

#### Classes

* Living (Default): This is the norm for most characters.
* Demon: The character has a darkness; yields advantages in nefarious ways.
* Deity: The character is god-like.
* Synthetic: The character experiences everything in a fabricated process.
* Digital: The character is at the mercy of ones and zeros.

#### Forms

* Feral: The character is the most rudimentary of the species, often wild and untamed.
* Human: The character is identifiable as human.
* Anthropomorphic (Default): The character exhibits human characteristics.
* Shapeshifter: The character has no exact form but defines or copies its form.
* Taur: The character has a human or anthropomorphic upper body attached to the four-legged body of an animal.
* Monster: The character is a legendary, fictional, or mythological creature that is often large, grotesque, and frightening, usually threatening the human world
